Our Programs

Two interconnected pillars for holistic development

Education Pathways

FPP

Future Pathways Program

The Future Pathways Program (FPP) is Pathways’ flagship, residential and highly selective 18-month program. It is designed for students who demonstrate strong academic readiness, discipline and capacity to benefit from intensive preparation for higher education.

APP

Access Pathways Program

Running concurrently with FPP is the Access Pathways Program (APP), a one-year program designed for students who demonstrate strong potential and alignment with Pathways’ values but are not selected for the resource-intensive FPP.

Community Pathways

Children Enrichment Pathway

The Children Enrichment Pathway is designed to support the holistic development of children in the local site community, focusing on early literacy, reading clubs, creative arts, and emotional growth.

Employment Pathway

This includes all salaried staff, managers and general workers directly employed by Pathways. These individuals are essential to the daily functioning of the organisation and the maintenance of its physical site.

Partnerships Pathway

Pathways recognises that sustainable impact requires collaboration. The Partnerships Pathways category governs all formal relationships with external individuals, institutions and organisations that contribute expertise, services, or resources to Pathways.

Small-Scale Enterprises Pathway

These enterprises are established and owned by Pathways but operated in collaboration with members of the local community. Their purpose is twofold: to provide livelihood opportunities for community members and to generate modest, ethical income streams that support the organisation’s mission.
